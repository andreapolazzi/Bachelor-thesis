These preliminary results obtained on a first split of the data are already promising: the model achieved an AUC = 0.9942.

The confusion matrix, although being calculated on a single split, shows a good behavior, concordant with previous results: a good, significant accuracy (0.988), perfect specificity (1.000) and high sensitivity (0.800), that together yield a good balanced accuracy (0.900).

These preliminary results on a single random split are very strong, but they should be interpreted cautiously until confirmed by repeated cross-validation.
